About

Terroir-driven wines, honestly made

Avant-Garde Wine & Spirits is a New York-based importer specializing in organic, biodynamic, and natural wines from France, Italy, Spain, and the US. We seek out producers who work with integrity in the vineyard and the cellar — people whose wines taste like where they come from.
